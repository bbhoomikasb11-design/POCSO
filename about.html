About Shield360

We are a tech-enabled child safety initiative empowering children, educators, and communities through interactive learning, awareness, and collaboration. Our platform aligns with the POCSO Act and focuses on protection, education, and action.

Gamified Quizzes

Interactive, child-friendly quizzes teach kids about personal safety, boundaries, and identifying inappropriate behavior. Gamification makes learning fun, memorable, and engaging, encouraging active participation and better retention.

Student & Teacher Dashboards

Custom dashboards track student learning progress while enabling teachers to monitor engagement and provide timely support. These tools foster continuous education and early interventions for child safety.

NGO Collaborations

We partner with NGOs on the ground to support at-risk children and communities. Together, we extend education, awareness, and real-world assistance to where it is needed the most.

CSR Partnerships

Corporate partners contribute to our mission through impactful CSR initiatives. We collaborate to develop customized outreach, digital campaigns, and on-ground programs focused on child safety and empowerment.

Helplines & Resources

By integrating national and regional child helplines, help is just a click away. Our platform provides direct access to support services, legal assistance, and confidential reporting options for children and parents alike.

Our Mission

We strive to build a safe and informed world for every child. Through education, collaboration, and digital innovation, we equip children with the knowledge and tools needed to protect themselves and confidently seek help.
