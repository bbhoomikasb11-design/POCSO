Recent POCSO Act Cases & Updates

Hosur School Abuse Case

In Hosur, Tamil Nadu, a 61-year-old private school owner, along with four others—including his wife and a teacher—was arrested under the POCSO Act for the sexual harassment of a 9-year-old girl.

Read More: The Times of India

YouTube: Times of India Channel

Moradabad Father Arrested

A 40-year-old man in Moradabad was booked under the POCSO Act for allegedly raping and impregnating his 15-year-old daughter. The investigation followed after the victim informed her mother.

Read More: The Times of India

YouTube: Times of India Channel

Nagpur Minor Pregnant After Rape

In Nagpur, a 16-year-old girl was found to be pregnant after abdominal pain complaints related to sexual assault by a classmate. The accused has been arrested by authorities.

Read More: The Times of India

YouTube: Times of India Channel

Kanpur Lawyer Charged with Extortion

A Kanpur lawyer and his associate were charged with extortion by filing a false rape case under the POCSO Act against a BJP leader. Police have substantiated the claims of extortion.

Read More: The Times of India

YouTube: Times of India Channel

Gender-Neutral Application of POCSO

The Karnataka High Court reaffirmed that the POCSO Act is gender-neutral, clarifying that both men and women can be tried for child sexual assault under this law.

Read More: NDTV

YouTube: NDTV Channel

Age of Consent Debate

The Supreme Court is reviewing petitions seeking to lower the age of consent from 18 to 16 years. The government resists these changes emphasizing protection of minors.
